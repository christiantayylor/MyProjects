# Vacation Planner — User Guide
**Plan, Book, and Track Your Perfect Trip**

---

## 1. Getting Started

Vacation Planner is a web application that helps you organize every aspect of a trip from a single dashboard. Open your browser and go to `http://localhost:3000` (or the hosted URL provided by your administrator).

The navigation bar at the top of every page gives you quick access to all sections of the app:

| Nav Link | What It Does |
|---|---|
| Trips | Your main dashboard. Create new trips and view all existing ones. |
| Bookings | Search and book hotels and flights for the currently active trip. |
| Excursions | Discover and add local activities and attractions to your trip. |
| Expenses | View a complete cost breakdown and generate a downloadable expense report. |

The Bookings, Excursions, and Expenses links always point to the trip you last viewed. After creating or selecting a trip, these links will automatically update to show that trip's data.

---

## 2. Creating a Trip

The Trips page is your starting point. All your existing trips are displayed as cards at the top of the page.

### How to create a new trip

1. Scroll down to the **Add a New Trip** card at the bottom of the Trips page.
2. In the **Destination** field, start typing a city name. A list of matching cities will appear — click one to select it.
3. Click the **Start Date** field and choose your departure date from the calendar.
4. Click the **End Date** field and choose your return date. This cannot be before the start date.
5. Click **Add Trip**. The new trip will appear as a card at the top of the page.

To view a trip's details, click anywhere on its card or the **View Trip** button. This takes you to the Bookings page for that trip.

To edit the destination or dates of a trip, click **Edit Trip** on the trip card.

To delete a trip, click **Edit Trip** and then **Delete Trip**. This permanently removes the trip and all associated bookings, excursions, and expenses.

## 3. Bookings

The Bookings page lets you search for and save hotel and flight bookings for your trip.

### Booking a Hotel

1. Under **Hotel Booking**, type the destination city in the Hotel Destination field.
2. Select your check-in and check-out dates.
3. Click **Find Hotel**. A list of available hotels will appear below.
4. Click a hotel card to select it (it will highlight). Then click **Book Hotel** to save it.

### Booking a Flight

1. Under **Transportation Booking**, enter your Departure City and Destination City.
2. Select the Departure Date.
3. Click **Find Transportation**. Available flights will appear in a scrollable list.
4. Click a flight card to select it, then click **Book Transportation**.

### Managing Bookings

All saved bookings appear in the table at the top of the Bookings page, showing the type, details, cost, and date. To remove a booking, click the **Delete** button in its row.

---

## 4. Excursions

The Excursions page lets you search for local activities and attractions powered by the Booking.com API, and add them to your trip.

### How to find and add an excursion

1. Under **Find Excursions**, type the city you want to explore.
2. Select the start and end dates of your excursion window.
3. Choose a **Sort By** option: Trending, Top Rated, or Lowest Price.
4. Click **Find Excursions**. Results appear below as cards showing the name, description, rating, duration, and price.
5. Click a card to select it (it will highlight). The name of the selected excursion appears next to the result count.
6. Click **Add to Trip** to save the excursion. It will appear in the Excursions table at the top of the page.

Click a different card to change your selection, or click the same card again to deselect it.

---

## 5. Expenses and Reports

The Expenses page automatically pulls together everything you have added to the trip — bookings, excursions, and manual expenses — into a single cost summary. No manual data entry is required for items already added through Bookings or Excursions.

### 5.1 Viewing the Expense Summary

The table at the top of the page shows all items grouped into three sections: **Bookings**, **Excursions**, and **Other Expenses**. A **Grand Total** row appears at the bottom. This view updates in real time whenever you add or remove items.

### 5.2 Generating a Report

1. Scroll to the **Generate Report** section.
2. Optionally enter a custom **Report Title**. If left blank, the title defaults to `[Destination] Expense Report`.
3. Use the checkboxes to select which categories to include. Check **All** to include everything, or select individual categories such as Hotel, Excursion, or Transportation.
4. Click **Generate Report**. The report appears below, showing a filtered table with a date-time stamp indicating when the report was produced.

### 5.3 Saving or Printing the Report

1. After generating the report, click **Save Report**.
2. Your browser's Print dialog will open. The report is formatted for clean black-and-white printing.
3. In the Destination dropdown (Chrome/Edge) or PDF button (Safari), choose **Save as PDF** to save the file to your device.
4. The default filename matches the report title you entered.

Click **Clear Report** to dismiss the generated report and start a new one with different settings.

## 6. Editing a Trip

To update a trip's destination or travel dates:

1. On the Trips page, find the trip card and click **Edit Trip**.
2. The Edit Trip page opens showing the current destination and dates.
3. To change the destination, clear the Enter City field and start typing a new city. Select from the autocomplete suggestions.
4. To change the dates, click the calendar fields and pick new dates.
5. Click **Update Trip** to save. You will be returned to the Trips page.

To delete the trip entirely, click **Delete Trip** on the Edit Trip page. A confirmation prompt will appear before the trip is permanently removed.

---

## 7. Tips and Notes

- **City autocomplete:** Type at least 2 characters in any destination field to trigger suggestions. Results are cached — if the same city is searched twice, the second result is instant.
- **Navbar links:** After viewing a trip, the Bookings, Excursions, and Expenses links in the top navbar will always point to that trip. Switch trips by going to the Trips page and clicking a different trip card.
- **Excursion dates:** The start and end dates on the Excursions search represent the window of your stay, not a specific date. The API returns all available activities within that range.
- **Report categories:** If you have added custom expense entries, those category names will appear as individual checkboxes in the report generator alongside the default Hotel, Transportation, and Excursion options.
- **PDF filename:** The browser will use your report title as the default PDF filename. Entering a specific title like `Miami 2026 Expenses` makes it easy to find later.
