About Christians Computer Shop

Thanks for visiting Christians Computer Shop. Founded in 2024 by Christian Taylor and based outside of Chicago, Illinois, Christians Computer Shop has solutions for all of your workstation needs. Whether you are looking for top-of-the-line reliable desktop PCs or laptops, such as HP and Dell, or accessories that you may be missing or in need of an upgrade, Christians Computer Shop will have what you are looking for.

Our devices are top-notch brand new devices that are shipped to customers through UPS Ground. Orders typically arrive to customers within 2-5 business days. All devices and accessories sold are new, perfect condition and guaranteed functional, so you do not need to worry about receiving any faulty products.

Our inventory management site allows you to see what products we have available, as well as how many of each product we are able to ship out. Orders are sent in order of purchase, and will usually be shipped out between 1-6 hours after ordered.

Our Vision

The vision at Christians Computer Shop is to provide customers with the ideal equipment needed to ensure that any workstation or gaming setup has every single item required to function flawlessly. Whether you already have equipment and need to order the headset and keyboard you forgot to order, or you are at the very beginning of your journey and need to start looking for the perfect device for your needs, Christians Computer Shop is here to support you at every step of your journey.
